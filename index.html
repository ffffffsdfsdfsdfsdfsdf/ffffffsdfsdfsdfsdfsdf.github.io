What is Extortion?

Extortion is the act of obtaining something, especially money, through force, threats, or intimidation. It is a serious crime that affects individuals and businesses worldwide.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Never respond to threats or pay extortionists.
  • Keep your personal information private and secure.
  • Report suspicious messages or calls to authorities.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for your accounts.
  • Educate friends and family about common scams.
